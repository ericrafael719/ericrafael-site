Flagship Collection

Bouquet of Words

A collection of spoken word pieces stitched together like flowers in a vase — some thorny, some soft, all chosen on purpose. Bouquet of Words is what happens when grief, gratitude, and growing up share the same page.

Each verse is an offering. The kind you hand to someone you love when you don't have the words to fix anything but want them to know you brought what you had.

"This isn't a book. It's a bouquet. Hold it carefully."
